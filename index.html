I received my B.Eng. and M.Eng. respectively in 2018 and 2021 from Beijing Institute of Technology (BIT), Beijing, China. I am currently pursing my Ph.D. in Control Science and Technology at the National Key Lab of Autonomous Intelligent Unmanned Systems, BIT, Beijing, China.

My current research interests include evolutionary computation, machine learning and operations research. I have authored 7 high-quality Research Papers in the field of computational intelligence, optimization, decision making, and their applications in vehicle routing problems, unmanned aerial vehicle swarm and air-ground coordination. I have applied 2 C.N. Patents. I participate in research projects supported by National Natural Science Foundations of China (NSFC), National Key Research and Development Program of China, etc.
